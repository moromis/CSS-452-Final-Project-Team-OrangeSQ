A Snowman's Nightmare

Our hero, Snowy the Snowman, is caught in the middle of a rain of fire. Put out the fire by spouting water.

Avoid the many types of fire and explosives and keep them from destroying the very ground you stand on, lest you fall through.

Stay alive as long as possible and get the best highscore! Enjoy!
