SnowMan's Nightmare

Our hero, Snowman, is caught in the middle of a rain of Fire. Put out the fire by spouting water. Beware! There is a deadly purple fire that can chase you. Putting out this fire will earn you double points. and re-populate the ice floor. Earn 5000 points to win the game. Catching the fire higher up will net you the most points. Enjoy!

Game Development Requirements:
1. More than 10 objects at any time on the Scene.
2. Three scene transition (Start Screen, Instruction Scene, Game Scene, Win Scene)
3. Inter Object Collision (Per-Pixel between fire and Snowman, Physics between Fire & Ice and Fire & Water)
4. At least two Cameras: Main Game Camera and Fire Focused collision Cameras - 4
5. Behaviors: Interpolate- Fire, Chase - Angry fire, Shake - Camera Shake
6. Physics: Snowman and Ice.
7. Lights: SpotLight - Fire, Directional Light - Background, SpotLight - Angry Fire.
8. Normal Map: Igloo and Background.
9. Extras: Particles
